Hello, we are Emofiz!!!
Emofiz is a character created based on the four basic emotions: joy, anger, love, and rock. Although they have different personalities, looks, and hobbies, they run a grocery store together and care for each other. Always bright and positive rabbit Willy, timid and sometimes depressed but lovely cat Saddy, playful and lovable panda Lavi, and finally a rather blunt-looking but warm-hearted dinosaur Angro.
